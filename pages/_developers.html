Eclipse M2M Industry Working Group

The Paho Project participates in Eclipse's Machine-to-Machine (M2M)Industry Working Group. The M2M Industry Working Group aims at defining an open development environment and key runtimes for M2M solutions that will enable open solutions for M2M and Internet of Things. If you are intersted in broader industry application of this technology or getting involved to drive new opprtunity and to accelerate growth of these types of applications, please checkout the Working Group's Charter , Wiki , or subscribe to the M2M IWG Mailing List
